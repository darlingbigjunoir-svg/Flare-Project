WHO WE ARE

Passion for Dance, Built for You

Flaire Dance Studio was founded with one mission: to make dance accessible, fun, and transformative for everyone. Whether you're stepping onto the dance floor for the first time or looking to sharpen your skills, our world-class instructors are here to guide every step.

We offer Hip Hop, K-Pop, and Modern Dance classes available both offline and online, designed to fit any lifestyle, schedule, or fitness goal.
