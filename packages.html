HAVE QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch packages after signing up?
Yes! You can upgrade or switch your package at any time. Our team will help you transition smoothly to a plan that fits your goals better.
Are the online classes live or pre-recorded?
Our online classes are conducted live so you get real-time feedback from our instructors. Recordings are also available for 48 hours after each session.
Do I need prior dance experience?
Not at all! All our packages are open to beginners and experienced dancers alike. We have beginner-friendly sessions built into every class schedule.
Is there a free trial before I commit?
Absolutely. We offer a free trial class for all new students. Head over to our Class Trial page to book your free session and experience Flaire for yourself.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards, bank transfers, and popular digital wallets. Monthly billing is automatic and can be cancelled at any time.
